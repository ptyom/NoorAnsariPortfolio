History

I first started coding in high school after participating in a Girls Who Code workshop for beginners interested in learning how to code.

I began learning HTML/CSS and JavaScript where I began my passion in writing code and developing websites.

My interest in Computer Science stems from the different ways I can solve a problem and use it to create something meaningful. I value working in a collaborative nature for this reason as I can build with others and offer one another different perspectives.

I am constantly trying to learn new technologies and concepts! I enjoy building projects to reflect my understanding and hopefully tackle a problem head on that needs to be addressed!

I am very dedicated to diversity and inclusion in the tech space so I try to volunteer my time and support initiatives targeted towards minorities and provide mentorship in any areas I can.
